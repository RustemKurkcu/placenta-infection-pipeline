# Fig07: DotPlot: glycocalyx / O-glycosylation proxies

## Hypothesis / idea being tested
Barrier/entry-relevant glycocalyx programs are enriched in specific trophoblast compartments (candidate susceptible niches).

## Methods (what we did)
DotPlot summarizing average expression (color) and percent expressing (dot size) of curated glyco/glycocalyx genes per cell type.

## Readout (what to look for)
Cell types with high mucin/proteoglycan/glycosyltransferase signal.

## Interpretation template
- Enrichment in trophoblast supports a susceptibility proxy.
- If mostly immune/stromal, refine the gene set or score within trophoblast only.
