# Fig06: Cell-type composition by infection

## Hypothesis / idea being tested
Some infections may alter survival/representation of specific compartments (immune enrichment, trophoblast depletion) or reflect selective infection of niches.

## Methods (what we did)
Count cells per infection × cell_type and convert to fractions per infection.

## Readout (what to look for)
Large shifts for a given infection suggest differential survival, dissociation bias, or true compositional remodeling.

## Interpretation template
- Validate with per-sample (donor) composition to rule out sampling artifacts.
- If stable, prioritize state changes (gene programs) rather than abundance changes.
