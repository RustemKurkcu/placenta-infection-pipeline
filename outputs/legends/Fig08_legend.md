# Fig08: DotPlot: adhesion proxies

## Hypothesis / idea being tested
Epithelial adhesion programs (CDH1/EPCAM/integrins) mark potential pathogen-binding/entry-permissive trophoblast states.

## Methods (what we did)
DotPlot of curated adhesion genes by cell type.

## Readout (what to look for)
Cell types with highest CDH1/EPCAM/integrin signal.

## Interpretation template
- High adhesion in trophoblast supports entry-susceptibility framing.
- If uniform, use pathogen-specific receptor sets.
