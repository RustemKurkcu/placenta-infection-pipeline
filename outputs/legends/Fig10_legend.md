# Fig10: UMAP overlay: AdhesionScore

## Hypothesis / idea being tested
Entry/attachment susceptibility varies across cell states; adhesion-high niches may be more permissive.

## Methods (what we did)
Compute an adhesion signature per cell and overlay on UMAP; use quantile cutoffs and ordering for clarity.

## Readout (what to look for)
Localized high-score regions (red) indicate putative entry-permissive states.

## Interpretation template
- If adhesion-high aligns with specific trophoblast subtypes, prioritize them for infection/DE analysis.
- If widespread, adhesion is not discriminatory; refine gene set.
